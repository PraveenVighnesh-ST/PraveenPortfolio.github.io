PaTS Wheel

The PaTS-Wheel maintains the energy efficiency and smooth ride characteristic of standard wheels on flat ground, with comparable energy consumption and vibrational profiles. Testing has shown a 100% success rate on obstacles up to approximately 70% of its diameter, significantly outperforming equivalent standard wheels (≈25%) and whegs (≈61%).

Raspberry Pi 4 Case

This enclosure is a two-part plastic case for the Raspberry Pi 4, featuring a solid base and a sliding top cover. The base includes precise cutouts for all ports, a side slot for easy microSD card access, and an opening for the camera module ribbon cable. The top cover slides open to expose the GPIO pins and camera port while keeping the rest of the board protected.
